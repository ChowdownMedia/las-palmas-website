Events

Parties, family nights, live moments, and local events from Las Palmas.

Cinco de Mayo Fiesta

Live mariachi, frozen lime margaritas all afternoon, and our signature tacos al pastor. Bring the whole family — this one’s always our biggest party of the year.

Locations: Shorter, Riverside

Karaoke Throwdown

Grab the mic and the queso. Half-price domestic drafts after 8pm. Prizes for the best — and bravest — performers of the night.

Location: Rockmart

Family Sunday Cookout

Kids eat free with every adult entrée. Face painting, balloon animals, and a build-your-own-nacho bar set up on the patio. Stop in any time after 11am.
