Las Palmas Riverside is a place for family dinners, lunch breaks, drinks with friends, and nights that feel a little more alive. With Mexican favorites, cold drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere, Riverside brings the Las Palmas experience to one of Rome's busiest local corridors.
